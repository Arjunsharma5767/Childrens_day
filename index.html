Tech Facts for Young Innovators

First Computer Programmer

The world's first computer programmer was Ada Lovelace, who wrote algorithms for Charles Babbage's Analytical Engine in the 1840s.

Young Tech Innovators

Many successful tech entrepreneurs started coding at a young age. Bill Gates wrote his first program at 13, and Mark Zuckerberg created a messaging app in middle school.

Internet Origins

The internet began as ARPANET, a project of the U.S. Department of Defense, with the first message sent in 1969 between two computers.

Future Technologies

Technologies like quantum computing, neural interfaces, and advanced AI that seem like science fiction today may become commonplace during your lifetime.
